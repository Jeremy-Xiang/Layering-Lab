What to Layer With Layton

Layton by Parfums de Marly is a regal apple-vanilla (fruity, gourmand, spicy). Here are its highest-scoring layering partners, ranked by shared note structure and scent-family chemistry.

Layering works when two fragrances share a bridge — a note both contain or two families perfumers blend together. Layton's fruity-leaning profile pairs best with the fragrances below. Apply the heavier one first, wait ~30 seconds, then the lighter one; start two sprays each and adjust.

Layton + Baccarat Rouge 540
92 match

Layton’s apple-vanilla gourmand core shares the amber-sweet register of BR540; the result amplifies both into a glowing, regal sweetness with absurd longevity.

Apply Baccarat Rouge 540 first (heavier base), wait ~30 seconds, then 1–2 sprays of Layton over or beside it. Start 2:2 and adjust.
