What to Layer With Baccarat Rouge 540

BR540 is the most-layered fragrance on earth. Here are the pairings that beat wearing it alone — scored and explained.

Baccarat Rouge 540 is sweet, mineral, and slightly thin on its own — which is exactly why it layers so well. It needs either depth underneath it or a creamy cushion around it. These four do the job; apply BR540 second, as the lighter top layer, in most cases.

Baccarat Rouge 540 + Ombré Leather
96 match

Ombré Leather supplies animalic leather depth BR540 lacks; BR540's glow softens the leather. The signature luxury BR540 combo.
